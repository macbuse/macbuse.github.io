Separation of variables

\(y' = \frac{dy}{dx}\)

Leibniz notation

  • \(\frac{dy}{dx}=y\)
  • \(\frac{dy}{y}=dx\)
  • \(\log(y) = x + C.\)

Applying the exponential

  • \(y = e^{\log(y)}= e^{(x + C)}.\)

  • \(y = e^Ce^x = Ae^x\)

and...

\(y(0)=1 \Rightarrow A=1\)

so finally..

\(y(x) = e^x\)
